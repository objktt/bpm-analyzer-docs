Privacy Policy for BPM Collect

Last updated: December 28, 2024

Introduction

Objktt Studio ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use BPM Collect (the "App").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address (when you sign in with Google or Apple)
  • Name (from your Google or Apple account)
  • Profile picture (from your Google or Apple account)

Usage Data

  • BPM measurements and vinyl record data
  • Search queries for Discogs integration
  • App usage statistics and preferences
  • Device information (model, operating system version)

Audio Data

  • Temporary audio recordings for BPM analysis (processed locally and not stored)
  • Microphone access is only used during active BPM measurement

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the BPM measurement service
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Integrate with Discogs for vinyl record information
  • Improve our app and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important updates about the service

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is securely stored using Supabase infrastructure
  • We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data
  • Audio recordings are processed locally and immediately deleted
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access

Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

Supabase

For secure data storage and user authentication

Discogs API

For vinyl record metadata and artwork

Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In

For secure authentication (optional)

Google AdMob

For displaying ads to free users (with personalized ad preferences)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Children's Privacy

BPM Collect is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
